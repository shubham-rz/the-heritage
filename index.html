Banganga Revival

Our First project in collaboration with MCGM (D Ward) and DoAM. The focus is on uplifting community spaces and spreading awareness about Banganga, rediscovering its heritage by augmenting experiences, improving accessibility of the site, and making heritage relevant for our future generation with collectibles.
